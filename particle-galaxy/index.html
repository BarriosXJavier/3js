Particle Galaxy

Controls:

Drag to rotate

Scroll to zoom

Space - Toggle auto-rotate

R - Reset camera

Click - Create ocean wave

Concepts Used:

  • BufferGeometry (100k particles)
  • Custom Shaders (GLSL)
  • Post-processing (Bloom)
  • PointsMaterial & ShaderMaterial
  • OrbitControls
  • Multiple render passes
  • Dynamic lighting
  • Tone mapping
  • Raycasting interaction
  • Wave propagation physics
